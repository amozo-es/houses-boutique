# Exclusive Golf Villa in Benahavís

Experience the perfect blend of mountain luxury and Mediterranean living in this stunning villa located within one of the most prestigious golf communities in Southern Spain.

## Golf Course Paradise

Nestled within the exclusive La Zagaleta golf estate, this villa offers unparalleled views of the championship golf course, surrounding mountains, and Mediterranean coastline. The residence provides direct access to one of Spain's most renowned golf experiences.

## Andalusian Heritage

The villa showcases traditional Andalusian architecture with modern luxury finishes. Authentic details such as terracotta tiles, wooden beams, and decorative ironwork blend seamlessly with contemporary amenities and state-of-the-art technology.

## Expansive Outdoor Living

The mature gardens create a private oasis with panoramic views. Multiple terraces and the infinity pool offer perfect settings for outdoor entertaining, while the private dining area allows for al fresco meals with breathtaking backdrops of the Costa del Sol.

## Exclusive Location

Benahavís offers a sophisticated mountain lifestyle while being just minutes from Marbella and Puerto Banús. The exclusive community provides privacy and security while maintaining easy access to world-class amenities, beaches, and international airports.
